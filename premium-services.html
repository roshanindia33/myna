Premium Plan FAQs

What is the difference between free and Premium Myna?

The free version of Myna provides basic investment tracking and access to standard investment options. Myna Premium unlocks advanced analytics, personalized advisory, priority support, exclusive opportunities, and tax optimization tools designed for more serious investors seeking accelerated growth and a consistent monthly income stream.

Can I switch between monthly and annual plans?

Yes, you can upgrade from a monthly to an annual plan at any time. Your remaining monthly subscription value will be adjusted against the annual plan fee. Downgrading from annual to monthly will take effect at the end of your current annual billing cycle, ensuring flexibility for your financial planning.

What kind of financial advisory can I expect?

Our financial advisors are SEBI-certified professionals who provide personalized investment advice based on your risk appetite, financial goals, and market conditions. They can help you craft a tailored portfolio, review existing investments, and guide you through complex financial decisions, ensuring your investments align with your aspirations.

Is there a refund policy for Premium subscriptions?

Our refund policy for Premium subscriptions is detailed in our Terms & Conditions. Generally, refunds are processed based on usage and the remaining subscription period. Please refer to the full policy for specific terms and conditions regarding cancellations and refunds, ensuring transparency and clarity.
