Frequently Asked Questions

What is Myna App?

Myna App is an innovative investment platform based in India that allows users to invest monthly and potentially earn high returns (5-15%) based on diversified investment strategies in stocks, trading, and other financial instruments. Our goal is to simplify wealth creation for everyone, offering a consistent monthly income stream from your investments.

How are the monthly returns generated?

Our expert team employs a diversified investment strategy, deploying funds across various sectors including high-growth Indian equities, carefully selected mutual funds, and active trading strategies. Returns are generated from the profits of these professionally managed portfolios, aiming for consistent monthly payouts.

Is my investment safe with Myna?

We prioritize the security of your investments and data. Myna adheres to SEBI-registered methodologies where applicable and employs advanced encryption and security protocols to protect your information. While all investments carry market risks, we strive to minimize these through diversified strategies and robust risk management and regulatory compliance.

What are the charges or fees?

At the end of each month, a small percentage (e.g., 0.5% tax and 0.15% to 0.2% transfer charges) is typically deducted from the profit earned before the remaining amount is transferred to your account. All fees are transparently disclosed in our Terms & Conditions. There are no hidden charges, ensuring complete transparency for our users.

How can I withdraw my funds?

You can request a withdrawal directly through the Myna app's intuitive interface. Withdrawals are processed based on our refund policy, typically within 2-3 business days, and the amount will reflect your portfolio's profit/loss status at the time of withdrawal. We aim for quick and hassle-free transactions, providing you access to your funds when you need them.

Do I need KYC to invest?

Yes, to ensure compliance with Indian financial regulations and for your security, all users are required to complete a Know Your Customer (KYC) process. This typically involves providing Aadhaar, PAN, and bank details. The process is quick, fully digital within the app, and essential for secure and compliant investment activities.

What is Myna Premium and how does it help?

Myna Premium offers advanced features like personalized financial advisory, deeper portfolio analytics, priority customer support, and exclusive investment opportunities. It's designed for serious investors looking for an edge and dedicated guidance to maximize their returns and achieve their financial goals faster.

How does Myna compare to traditional investment apps like Groww?

While apps like Groww focus on direct investments in mutual funds and stocks, Myna offers a managed portfolio approach with a focus on potentially higher monthly returns through active strategies. We aim to provide a more hands-off experience with expert management, targeting a specific return range not typically offered by self-service platforms, and providing a consistent monthly income feel.

What kind of support can I expect?

We offer comprehensive customer support via email and in-app chat. Premium users receive priority support with dedicated channels. Our team is committed to resolving your queries promptly and efficiently, ensuring you always have the assistance you need.

What are the minimum and maximum investment amounts?

The minimum monthly investment amount is ₹1,000, and the maximum is ₹100,000 for standard users. These limits are set to accommodate a wide range of investors while managing our portfolio efficiently. For higher investments, please contact our premium advisory team to discuss tailored solutions.

How often can I invest?

You can set up monthly recurring investments (SIP-like) or make one-time lump-sum investments at your convenience through the app. Our system is flexible to suit your investment frequency preferences, giving you full control over your investment schedule.

How does Myna ensure high returns?

Our high returns are a result of a meticulously crafted strategy involving diversified portfolio management, advanced algorithmic trading, and expert market analysis. We focus on identifying high-growth opportunities while employing robust risk mitigation techniques to protect your capital and maximize potential gains.

Can I track my investments in real-time?

Yes, the Myna app provides a comprehensive dashboard where you can track your investments, monitor performance, view your returns history, and get real-time updates on your portfolio's value. Premium users get even more in-depth analytics and customized reports.
