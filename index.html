How to Download & Install

1

Download the APK

Click the "Download Now" button above. The app's APK file will start downloading to your device.

Go to Download Button

2

Enable Unknown Sources

Before installing, you might need to allow installations from "Unknown Sources" in your Android settings. This is a standard security prompt for apps not downloaded from Google Play Store.

Go to Settings > Security/Privacy > Install unknown apps and enable it for your browser or file manager.

Important Note: This warning is standard for all apps downloaded outside the Play Store. Myna is 100% safe and secure. Enabling "Unknown Sources" temporarily for our app will not harm your device.
3

Install the App

Once the download is complete, open the downloaded APK file from your notifications or file manager. Follow the on-screen instructions to install Myna on your device.

After installation, you can disable "Unknown Sources" if you wish.
